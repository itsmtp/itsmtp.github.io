Mid-Term Examination Schedule — Spring 2026

📅 24 March 2026 🏛 Examination Department

Dear Students,


The Examination Department is pleased to announce that the mid-term examination schedule for Spring 2026 has been officially released.


Examinations will commence from Monday, April 5, 2026 and will continue through April 12, 2026. All students are required to download their hall tickets from the student portal no later than April 3rd.


Please ensure you carry a valid university ID card and your printed hall ticket on examination day. Any student found without these documents will not be permitted to sit the exam.


Students with exam scheduling conflicts should report to the Examination Department immediately.


Best Regards,
